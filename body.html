Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +

Metro Manila deliveries typically take 1-2 business days. Provincial deliveries may take 3-7 business days depending on the location. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 12 PM in Metro Manila.
Can I return or exchange a toy? +

Yes! We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened and unused toys in their original condition and packaging. Just bring it to any Toy Kingdom store or contact our support team.
How do I set up a Birthday Registry? +

You can create a Birthday Registry through your Account Dashboard or by visiting the customer service desk in any physical store. You'll receive a unique link to share with family and friends!
Do you offer bulk/school orders? +

Absolutely. We provide special discounts for schools, orphanages, corporate events, and charities. Please use the contact form and select "Bulk Orders" to get a customized quote.
Is my payment information secure? +

We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption to process all transactions. We accept GCash, Maya, major credit cards, and Cash on Delivery. Your financial data is never stored on our servers.
