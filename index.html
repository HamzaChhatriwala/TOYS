How to Choose the Right RC Car for Your Child's Age

RC cars are one of the most gifted toys in India — but picking the right one by speed, range and battery life can be tricky. Here's our complete guide...

Ages 3–5: Choose slow-speed RC cars with chunky controls and durable bodies. Avoid very small parts. Battery-operated push-cars are ideal.

Ages 6–8: 2.4GHz RC cars with 15–20 km/h speed are perfect. Look for rechargeable batteries (not replaceable) for frequent use.

Ages 9+: High-speed drift RC cars (30+ km/h), wall-climbing cars, and camera-equipped RC cars are big hits. Gel blaster combos are also trending.

Pro tip: At Toy Kingdom's Nagdevi Street store, you can test RC cars before buying — something online stores can't offer!

Top 5 Educational Toys for Kids Under 5 in 2025

India's parents are now choosing toys that teach — from piano keyboards to dinosaur puzzles. Here are 5 must-have toys for early childhood development...

1. Animal Piano Keyboards — Cat, cow and dino pianos (available at Toy Kingdom) develop sound recognition and fine motor skills.

2. Push & Go Teddies — Cause-and-effect learning through tactile play.

3. DIY Assembly Kits — Builds patience, spatial reasoning and creativity.

4. Battery-Operated Dancing Animals — Encourages rhythm sense and imitation learning.

5. Train Track Sets — Hand-sensor track sets develop logical sequencing skills.

Nagdevi Street — Mumbai's Toy Wholesale Hub Explained

If you're a retailer or just a toy enthusiast, Nagdevi Street in Mumbai is the epicentre of India's toy trade. Here's everything you need to know about this legendary market...

Where is Nagdevi Street? Located in the bustling Mohammad Ali Road area of South Mumbai, Nagdevi Street is one of India's oldest and most active wholesale toy markets. It has been a trading hub for decades.

What can you find here? Everything from battery-operated toys, die-cast models, RC cars, dolls, educational toys, guns, trains — at prices that are 40–60% lower than retail MRP.

Who shops here? Small and large toy retailers from across India, event planners, school supply agencies, and even international buyers source products from here.

Toy Kingdom's role: Established in 2020, Toy Kingdom is based right on Nagdevi Street and has become a trusted name in the wholesale toy supply chain — serving Pan-India retailers with quality, variety, and speed.

📊 Industry Report

India's Toy Industry: ₹20,000 Crore Market & Growing Fast

India's toy sector is booming thanks to government push, rising middle-class spending, and curbs on Chinese imports. Here's a deep dive into the numbers...

Market Size: India's toy market is valued at approximately ₹20,000 crore (USD ~2.5 billion) and is projected to grow at 12–15% CAGR through 2030.

China Import Restrictions: Following the 2020 quality-control orders and higher import duties, Chinese toy imports dropped by over 70%. This created massive opportunity for domestic manufacturers and distributors.

Make in India push: The government's PLI scheme for toys and the Toy Clusters in Rajasthan and Karnataka have boosted local manufacturing significantly.

Consumer trends: Urban Indian parents are spending more on educational and STEM toys. Battery-operated, RC, and role-play toys are the fastest growing segments.

Mumbai's role: Mumbai (primarily Nagdevi Street, Dharavi, and Bhiwandi) remains the #1 distribution hub for toys across India, accounting for over 35% of total toy wholesale trade.

Source: FICCI Toy Report 2024, Ministry of Commerce data.

🇮🇳 Made in India
⏱ 5 min read

Made in India Toys: Why Local is the New Smart Buy

The shift from Chinese imports to Indian-manufactured toys is not just patriotic — it's practical. Better quality control, safer materials, and pan-India availability...

Quality assurance: Indian toys must now comply with BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) IS 9873 safety norms. This ensures no toxic materials and better build quality.

Price advantage: Made-in-India toys have eliminated the import markup. A die-cast car that used to cost ₹499 imported now retails at ₹249–₹299 locally produced.

Supply chain speed: Local sourcing means faster restocking. Wholesalers like Toy Kingdom can restock within 3–5 days versus 4–6 weeks for imports.

Key manufacturing hubs: Ahmedabad, Rajkot (Gujarat), Aligarh (UP), and increasingly Mumbai-Thane corridor are India's toy manufacturing powerhouses.

Our commitment: Toy Kingdom actively sources from Indian manufacturers and helps connect Pan-India retailers with domestic suppliers at the best wholesale rates.
